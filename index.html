About me

Wangli He received the B.Sc. degree in information and computing science and the Ph.D. degree in applied mathematics from Southeast University, Nanjing, China, in 2005 and 2010, respectively. She is currently a Professor with the School of Information Science and Engineering, East China University of Science and Technology, Shanghai, China. From 2010 to 2017, she held several visiting positions with Central Queensland University, Rockhampton, QLD, Australia, The University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, the City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, the Potsdam Institute for Climate Research Institute, Potsdam, Germany, and Tokyo Metropolitan University, Tokyo, Japan. Her research interests include distributed cooperative control and optimization, multi-agent games, networked multi-agent systems, autonomous intelligent systems, and electricity-hydrogen-carbon integrated energy systems.

Dr. He was a recipient of the Sixth Young Scientist Award of Chinese Association of Automation in 2020, and the first prize of Shanghai Natural Science Award in 2019. From 2018 to 2019, she was the Chair of the Technical Committee on Networked-based Control Systems and Applications of IES. She is an Associate Editor for several international journals including the IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics and the IEEE Journal of Emerging and Selected Topics in Industrial Electronics.

Research Interests

My research interests include

  • Swarm intelligence

  • Distributed control, optimization and learning for multi-agent systems

  • Electricity-hydrogen-carbon integrated energy systems

  • Multi-robot cooperative technology (SLAM, task allocation, motion planning, etc.)

  • Multi-agent trustworthy decision-making (non-cooperative games, reinforcement learning)
